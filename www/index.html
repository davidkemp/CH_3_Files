Twin Fall's Finest Salon Since 2014

Established in 2014, The Rustic Pear Salon has showcased the Jerome Valley, Twin Falls community's finest in fashionable styling and all forms of beauty treatments.

Rustic Pear Welcomes You !!!

We promise you’ll feel relaxed and youthful and look great after time spent at Rustic Pear Salon & Skin Spa! Rustic Pear Salon is proud to be a full-service Aveda salon. We are the salon other hair salons aspire to be, offering the latest trends in hair cuts, hair coloring, hair extensions and beauty treatments across the board. Our award-winning services put us in the top echelon of salons in the Valley. Visit us in Twin Falls for the salon experience even the trendiest in the Jerome valley are blown away by.

Rustic Pear Salon is proud to be set apart from the valley's salons both literally and figuratively. The atmosphere at Rustic Pear Salon is ever-changing and unlike any Twin Falls hair salon you’ll encounter. We aim for a unique, individualized experience, and as an Aveda Lifestyle Salon we offer certified organic products to go along with our award-winning services. From hair cutting and coloring techniques to aromatherapy and reflexology, you’ll find it all at Rustic Pear Salon.

Our full service Aveda salon features talented hair stylists, aestheticians, nail technicians and massage therapists offering:

• Hairstyling by award-winning, advanced educated, artistic team of hairstylists including blow dry style, women’s hairstyles, children’s haircuts, hair extensions, hair treatments, bridal and special events

• Hair Coloring that is naturally derived from plants, leaving hair essentially damage-free

• Hair texturing including Perms and Relaxers

• Hair Extensions including Platinum Seamless and Flash Point

• Brazilian Gloss Keratin Smoothing Treatment

• Full-Service Nail Salon including Natural Nails, Shellac, Acrylic and Gel

• Make-up Application & Lessons including Special Occasion and Bridal makeup

• Full Service Skin Spa including Facials, Waxing and Massage

Twin Fall's Finest Salon Since 2014


What a fun inaugural year 2014 was for RUSTIC PEAR SALON!!!

As the ownership of Rustic Pear Salon, we bring a combination 42 years of experience and training in every aspect of beauty and style treatments. Our business experience helps us to focus on the needs and desires of our clients. It was our desire to bring the finest professionals in the business into a team environment that produces a familial response to our customers and community.

We took a leap of faith and opened a salon in Downtown Twin Falls, knowing the renovations that will be taking place on Main Street will be an amazing atmosphere for many to enjoy!!!

We could not have done it without husbands, family members, friends, and our loyal clientele who saw our vision and were there to lend a helping hand! It's hard to mention all the blessings that have come from this adventure, but our fabulous Beauticians, Nail Techs, and Stylists have been AWESOMELY amazing and great team players!

2015 has all the hallmarks of being an amazing year for the salon and our customers! Thank you to all our clients who continue to support our professionals.

Plese know that we here at Rustic Pear Salon are so thankful for you and the Jerome Valley community !!!
